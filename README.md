# Healthcare Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
End-to-end Healthcare Analytics project developed using SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau to analyze patient demographics, clinical operations, treatment outcomes, diagnosis trends, and healthcare revenue performance. The project helps healthcare stakeholders monitor operational efficiency, patient care quality, and financial performance through interactive dashboards and KPI-driven reporting.

- Data Modeling
- Pivot Tables

- KPI Analysis

## Key KPIs
- Total Patients: 10,000
- Total Visits: 10,000
- Completion Rate: 32.66%
- Success Rate: 33.01%
- Revenue Generated: $2.54 Million
- Chronic Disease Rate: 80.35%
- Follow-Up Rate: 49.84%
- Cancellation Rate: 33.44%

## Key Insights
- Chronic Disease Rate reached 80.35%, indicating significant long-term care demand.
- Migraine was the most frequently diagnosed medical condition.
- Specialist consultations represented the largest visit category.
- Healthcare visits peaked during 2024.
- General Medicine, Pediatrics, and Cardiology were major revenue contributors.
- Hypertension emerged as the leading chronic condition.
- California and Texas recorded high patient concentrations.
- Follow-up visits accounted for a significant portion of healthcare operations.
- Revenue exceeded $2.54 Million across healthcare services.
- Dashboard insights support operational planning, resource allocation, and patient care optimization.
